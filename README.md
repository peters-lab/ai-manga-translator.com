Manga localization has long been a privilege for big publishers. For indie creators, the cost of professional translation and typesetting is prohibitive. **AI Manga Translator** is built to change that.

## 🛠️ Technical Prowess

This isn't just another text-to-text wrapper. We've engineered a specific pipeline for the unique challenges of manga:

- **Precision OCR:** Specifically tuned for vertical, stylized, and non-linear text bubbles.
- **Contextual Awareness:** Our engine understands narrative flow, not just isolated sentences.
- **Modern Infrastructure:** Powered by **Next.js 14 (App Router)** and **SSR** for high-performance image processing.
- **Automated Typesetting (Beta):** Intelligent font-scaling and balance maintenance within original balloons.
